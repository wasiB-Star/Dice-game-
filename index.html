How to Play: (show)

  • To start, click the 'Roll Dice' button.
  • To advance to the next round, click the 'Roll Dice' button again.
  • After three rounds, the game is over and the final score is calculated.

Rules: (show)

  • If any of the player's two dice comes up as a 1 then the score for that round for the player is 0.
  • If the player rolls a pair of the same numbers then the player's score is the total of the two dice times 2.
  • If the player rolls any other combination of dice, the player's score is the total value of the two dice.
